Privacy

Local music should stay local.

Dkompos is designed around a local-first music library. Your audio files, metadata, analysis, listening history, crates, and sets live on your Mac unless you choose to move or export them.

Library data

Dkompos scans local folders you choose and stores library data locally. The product is not designed around uploading your collection to a remote service.

Analysis

BPM, key, mood, audio quality, loudness, and recommendation data are generated for your library so Dkompos can help you listen, curate, and prepare sets. Analysis runs on your Mac.

Fonts

This site uses Bunny Fonts, a privacy-focused web-font host that does not log requests, set cookies, or build profiles.

Contact

Feedback sent through the website goes to the Dkompos inbox. If you include a reply email, it is used only to respond to your note. You can also email hello@dkompos.com directly.
